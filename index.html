About Bing Bin

It is difficult to sort!


Do you know where should we throw a dirty yogurt pot? In France 2016, the cost of wrong sort is 50 millions euro, the cost of non sort is 160 million euro. If everyone sort correctly in France, we will have a gain of 310 million euro. We all know the importance and the advantages of sorting but why is sorting still a global issue? Beause it is difficult to remember all the rules, because we can not see the direct impact of sorting.

Sorting rules are all different!


Sorting rules are vary from countries to countries. In Scandinavian, there are usually seven types of trash. In France, there are at most 4 types. Sorting rules are not uniform even in one country, each city has its own rules according to its recyclaling industries.








Green trashbin or yellow trash bin?


A survey has been conducted in France 2017, 230 students have responsed. 92% of students think sorting is important and more than 50% of them think the disposing system should be improved. The main reason that people don’t sort correctly because they don’t know how to sort.



Bing Bin recognizes your trash and proposes the perfect sort. Events, games, DIY... Sorting has never been that easy and fun with Bing Bin.
